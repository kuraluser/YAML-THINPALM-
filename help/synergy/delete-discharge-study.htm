Delete Discharge Study

To delete an existing discharge study, perform the steps given below:

  1. Navigate to CARGO PLANNING > Discharge Study.

    The Discharge Study List corresponding to the current active voyage appears by default. 

    To view the discharge study list created against another voyage, select the required option from the Voyage No drop-down list. 
  2. Click the drop-down icon () against the discharge study name you want to delete. 
  3. Click DELETE. 
  4. A pop-up appears prompting the user to confirm the action. Click Yes to proceed. 

    The Discharge Study page appears displaying a success message indicating the discharge study is successfully deleted.

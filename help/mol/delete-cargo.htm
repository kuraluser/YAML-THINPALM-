Delete Cargo

Perform the following steps to delete an existing cargo from a loadable study:

  1. From CARGO PLANNING, click Loadable Study. 

    By default, the page displays the loadable studies created against the current active voyage.

    To view and edit the loadable study created against another voyage, from the Voyage No drop-down, select the required option. 
  2. From the Loadable Study List table, select the required loadable study. 

    The CARGO NOMINATION tab appears.
  3. Click   > Delete to delete the cargo. 
    A pop-up appears prompting you to confirm the action. Click YES to procee

Deleting a cargo effects the selected ports, commingled data, OHQ, OBQ, and Synoptical Table.

Synoptical

The SYNOPTICAL page, by default, displays the voyage details of the current voyage. The Synoptical Table auto-populates the planned and actual values entered by the user while generating the loadable plan for the respective voyage. The user is allowed to enter the values during the voyage and during the loading and discharging operations. This data is populated on the vessel dashboard.

The Synoptical Table displays the following loadable study details of the current voyage :

Field Description

Port Name

The name of the ports included in the voyage. 

Distance

The distance between the ports included in the loadable study mapped against the voyage.

Speed

The average speed of the vessel during the voyage.

Running Hours

The time duration for the vessel to run between two ports in the port rotation.

In Port Hours

The time of stay of the vessel at each port.

ETA/ETD Date

The estimated date of arrival and departure of the vessel at each port.

ETA/ETD Time

The estimated time of arrival and departure of the vessel at each port.

Sunrise Time

The time of sunrise at each port.

Sunset Time

The time of sunset at each port.

Tide Level HW 

The From and To values of high water level during the tide at each port.

Tide Level LW

The From and To values of low water level during the tide at each port. 

Hog/Sag

The value of hogging and sagging at each port. Hog/Sag details are hidden for the vessels that do not have Loadicator. 

Draft

The fore draft, after draft, and mid-ship draft values of the vessel at each port. 

Trim

The trim condition of the vessel at each port.

Cargo MT

The total quantity of onboard cargo at each port during the arrival and departure. 

Fuel Oil

The quantity of onboard fuel oil at each port. 

Diesel Oil

The quantity of onboard diesel oil at each port.

Ballast 

The quantity of onboard ballast at each port.

Fresh Water

The quantity of onboard fresh water at each port. 

Others

The quantity of other remains on board including the crew, temporary fixtures, etc., at each port.

Constant

The constant quantity on board including permanent fixtures, machinery, etc.  

Total DWT

The total deadweight of the vessel at each port. Deadweight is the maximum amount of weight the vessel can carry.

Displacement

The total weight of the vessel at each port. This value is calculated by adding the deadweight and the lightweight of the vessel. 

Seawater Specific Gravity

The specific gravity of seawater at each port. The default value is 1.025. 

To view the loadable study details of another voyage on the Synoptical Table, select the required options from the Voyage, Loadable Study, and Loadable Pattern drop-downs.

Click Edit to edit the Synoptical Table. For more information, see Edit Synoptical Table.

To navigate to the respective loadable study page of the voyage, click Back to Loadable Study.

Delete Discharge Study

Perform the following steps to delete an existing discharge study:

  1. From the menu bar, click CARGO PLANNING > Discharge Study.

    The Discharge Study List corresponding to the active voyage appears by default. 

    To view the discharge study list created against another voyage, from the Voyage No drop-down, select the required option. 
  2. Click  > Delete  to delete the discharge study name. A pop-up appears, prompting the user to confirm the action. Click Yes to proceed. 
    The Discharge Study page appears, displaying a success message indicating the discharge study is deleted successfully.
